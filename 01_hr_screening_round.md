## Purpose of This Stage

The HR/screening round tests:
- Basic qualification fit
- Motivation and enthusiasm
- Cultural alignment signals
- Communication and professionalism
- Logistics viability (salary, start date, location)

**Interviewer Profile:** Talent acquisition professional. Has IT awareness but won't go deep on technical. Evaluates cultural fit, communication, enthusiasm.

**Success Criteria:** Advance to hiring manager/technical round. Leave positive impression.

## HR Round Q&A Categories

**Category A: Motivation**
- "Why are you looking now?"
- "Why this company?"
- "Why this role?"
- "What do you know about us?"

**Category B: Experience Validation**
- "Walk me through your background"
- "Tell me about your experience with [X]"
- "What's your biggest achievement?"

**Category C: Cultural Fit**
- "How do you handle [situation]?"
- "Describe your ideal work environment"
- "What management style works best for you?"

**Category D: Logistics**
- "What are your salary expectations?"
- "When can you start?"
- "Are you interviewing elsewhere?"

## HR Round Tips

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Show genuine enthusiasm | Be overly casual |
| Research the interviewer | Wing it |
| Prepare logistics answers | Dodge salary questions entirely |
| Ask about next steps | Have zero questions |
| Keep answers concise (30 min call) | Ramble |
| Connect experience to role | Be generic |
